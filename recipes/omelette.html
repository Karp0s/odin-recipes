Everything Omelette

A hearty breakfast full of protein and nutrients.

You can fill this omelette with virtually anything you like. From bacon to vegetables, the choice is up to you.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • A tablespoon of margarine
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • Filler of your choice. I'll be using tomatoes, onions and peppers
  • Some shredded Cheese
  • Ketchup

Directions

  1. Break and beat the eggs in a bowl
  2. Cut up your filler into small bite sized pieces. Add into bowl with salt and beat well
  3. Set a pan on the stove over medium heat. Throw the margarine in the pan once it is hot and allow it to coat the frying pan
  4. Pour your egg mixture into the pan and cook for 5-8 minutes until the egg is cooked
  5. Add the cheese on top of your omellete and fold it in half with your spatula, trapping the filler and cheese inside.
  6. Pour some ketchup over the omelette for taste and eat warm. Enjoy!
