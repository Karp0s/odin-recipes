Banana Shake

A smooth and sweet delight to beat those hot, sunny days!

This milkshake isn't your ordinary run of the mall, milkshake. Its creamier, its smoother, its more refreshing, and best of all?

YOU DONT GOTTA GO TO THE MALL TO GET IT. You'll just need a blender and a few things from around your kitchen.

Here's what you're gonna need:

  • A blender
  • A banana
  • 1 cup of ice-cream
  • 1.5 cups of milk
  • A few ice cubes

Here's what you gotta do:

  1. Add the banana, ice-cream and milk into the blender.
  2. Begin blending until all ingredients are blended well.
  3. Serve in a glass on ice, with a straw.
